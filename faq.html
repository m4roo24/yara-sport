Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Kurd Sport.

Last Updated: July 11, 2026
How do I start watching?
Simply click on any channel from the sidebar list. The stream will load automatically. For best performance, we recommend a stable internet connection of at least 5 Mbps.
Why is the stream buffering or not loading?
Streams may buffer due to slow internet, server overload, or channel being offline. Try switching to another channel or check your connection. Some channels use a proxy (marked with PROXY badge) which may take longer to connect.
Can I use this on my phone or TV?
Yes! Kurd Sport works on all devices: mobile phones, tablets, laptops, desktops, and Smart TVs. Install it as a PWA for the best experience. On TV, use your remote arrow keys to navigate and OK to select.
How do I install Kurd Sport as an app?
On Android/Chrome: tap the install banner at the bottom or use the menu → "Add to Home Screen." On iOS Safari: tap Share → "Add to Home Screen." On desktop Chrome/Edge: click the install icon in the address bar. This gives you a full-screen app experience without browser UI.
Can I add my own channels?
Yes! Open Settings (gear icon) and scroll to "Add Your Channel." Paste any M3U8 stream URL, give it a name, select a category, and click Add. Your custom channels are saved locally and persist across sessions.
What is Premium and how do I get it?
Premium removes all advertisements and gives you a faster, cleaner experience. Click the crown icon in the header, enter your premium code (format: YARA-XXXX-XXXX), and click Confirm. Premium is just $1/month.
Why do I see ads?
Ads help keep Kurd Sport free for everyone. If you see an ad-block warning, disable your ad blocker or consider upgrading to Premium for an ad-free experience. We never collect personal data through ads.
What controls work on TV?
All TV remote buttons are supported: D-Pad to navigate, OK/Enter to select, Back to close panels, CH+/- to change channels, Play/Pause for playback, and colored buttons (Red=Settings, Green=Fullscreen, Yellow=Mute, Blue=Controls Guide).
Can I cast to my TV?
Yes! Click the Cast icon (TV symbol) in the header. Make sure your Chromecast or Android TV is on the same WiFi network. Note: Casting requires Chrome or Edge browser and may not work with all channels.
How do I change the theme?
Open Settings and look for "Theme Color" to pick your accent color, or "Theme Mode" to switch between Dark, Light, and Auto (follows system preference). Your choice is saved automatically.
What are the keyboard shortcuts?
Space = Play/Pause, F = Fullscreen, M = Mute, P = Picture-in-Picture, N/→ = Next Channel, P/← = Previous Channel, S = Settings, R = Virtual Remote, 0-9 = Channel numbers. Open Settings → Controls Guide for the full list.
