Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Kurd Sport handles your information.

Last Updated: July 11, 2026

Information We Collect

Kurd Sport is designed with privacy in mind. We minimize data collection:

  • Local Storage: We use your browser's localStorage to save app preferences (theme color, custom channels, premium status). This data never leaves your device.
  • Device ID: A random anonymous ID is generated for premium verification purposes only.
  • Streaming Data: Channel URLs are processed through our proxy server solely to enable playback. We do not log viewing history.
No Personal Data Required

We do not require registration, email, phone number, or any personal information to use Kurd Sport.

How We Use Your Data

Any data stored is used exclusively for:

  • Remembering your app settings and preferences
  • Storing your custom-added channels
  • Validating premium membership status
  • Improving streaming performance through caching

Cookies & Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies or analytics that identify you personally. Third-party advertising partners may use cookies in accordance with their own policies. Premium users enjoy an ad-free experience with no third-party tracking.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Clear your browser data to remove all locally stored preferences
  • Contact us to request premium data deletion
  • Use the app in incognito/private mode for temporary sessions
