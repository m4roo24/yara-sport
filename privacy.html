Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 27, 2026

Privacy First: Yara Sport TV is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data, do not track you across the web, and do not sell your information to anyone. Your viewing activity remains private.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Yara Sport TV ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our web application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By using the Service, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect very limited information:

  • Local Storage Data: We use your browser's localStorage to save your preferences (theme color, theme mode, and any custom channels you add). This data is stored entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Usage Data (Optional): If enabled, basic anonymous usage statistics may be collected to help us improve the Service. This does not include any personally identifiable information.
  • Server Logs: Our hosting provider may automatically collect standard server logs including your IP address, browser type, access times, and pages visited. This is standard practice for all websites and is used solely for security and troubleshooting purposes.

What we DO NOT collect: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, payment information, or any other personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily contact us via email.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

Yara Sport TV uses localStorage (not traditional cookies) to store your preferences locally on your device. This includes:

  • Your selected theme color
  • Your preferred theme mode (dark/light/auto)
  • Any custom channels you have added

This data never leaves your browser. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site.

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

4. How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used solely for:

  • Providing and maintaining the Service
  • Remembering your preferences (theme, custom channels)
  • Improving the Service based on anonymous usage patterns
  • Detecting, preventing, and addressing technical issues or security threats
  • Responding to your inquiries when you contact us

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL.
