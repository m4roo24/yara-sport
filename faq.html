Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Yara Sport TV

Getting Started
How do I use Yara Sport TV? POPULAR

Using Yara Sport TV is simple:

  • Open the app and you'll see the player on the left and the channel list on the right.
  • Click on any channel from the Live Channels list to start streaming.
  • The stream will automatically load in the video player.
  • Use the controls at the bottom to play/pause, adjust volume, change quality, or go fullscreen.
  • You can filter channels by category using the tabs above the channel list.

No account or registration is required — just open and watch!

What devices and browsers are supported?

Yara Sport TV works on any modern device with a web browser:

  • Desktop: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (latest versions)
  • Mobile: iOS Safari, Android Chrome, Samsung Internet
  • Tablets: iPad, Android tablets
  • TV: Smart TVs with web browsers, Chromecast, AirPlay

For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of Chrome or Safari. HLS streaming requires a browser that supports it natively or via the HLS.js library (which we include).

Is Yara Sport TV free to use?

Yes, Yara Sport TV is completely free to use. There are no subscription fees, no hidden charges, and no premium tiers. We are committed to providing free access to live sports streaming.

However, please note that we rely on third-party stream sources, and the availability of streams is beyond our control.

Streaming
Why is my stream not loading? POPULAR

There are several reasons a stream might not load:

  • Channel Offline: The source stream may be temporarily offline or permanently removed.
  • Network Issues: Check your internet connection. A stable connection of at least 5 Mbps is recommended for HD streams.
  • Browser Compatibility: Try a different browser or update your current one.
  • CORS/Proxy Issues: Some channels require a proxy to bypass browser restrictions. The app auto-detects this, but you can also try toggling connection modes in Settings.
  • Ad Blockers: Some ad blockers may interfere with stream loading. Try disabling them temporarily.

If a specific channel consistently fails, try another channel or check back later — stream URLs change frequently.

How do I fix buffering or lagging?

If you're experiencing buffering or lag:

  • Lower the Quality: Click the AUTO button in the player controls and select a lower resolution (e.g., 480p or 720p instead of 1080p).
  • Check Your Connection: Run a speed test. You need at least 3 Mbps for SD and 5+ Mbps for HD.
  • Close Other Tabs/Apps: Free up bandwidth by closing other streaming services, downloads, or heavy apps.
  • Use a Wired Connection: If possible, use an Ethernet cable instead of Wi-Fi for more stable speeds.
  • Clear Browser Cache: Sometimes cached data can cause playback issues.
What is Proxy mode vs Direct mode? NEW

Yara Sport TV supports two connection modes:

  • Direct: Connects straight to the stream source. This is the fastest option but may be blocked by CORS (Cross-Origin Resource Sharing) or mixed-content policies in your browser.
  • Proxy: Routes the stream through our server to bypass CORS and mixed-content restrictions. Slightly slower but more reliable for blocked streams.

The app auto-detects the best mode for each channel. You can see the current connection mode in the channel info bar below the player.

How do I change the video quality?

To change video quality:

  • Hover over the player to reveal the controls.
  • Click the AUTO button in the bottom-right corner of the player.
  • Select your preferred resolution (e.g., 1080p, 720p, 480p) or leave it on AUTO to let the player adapt to your connection speed.

Note: Not all streams offer multiple quality options. If only one quality is available, the menu will show only AUTO.

How do I use Fullscreen or Picture-in-Picture?

Fullscreen: Click the expand icon (bottom-right of the player) or press the F key on your keyboard.

Picture-in-Picture (PiP): Click the clone icon (next to the fullscreen button) or press the P key. This lets you watch the stream in a small floating window while using other apps.

On mobile, you can also use your device's native fullscreen gesture (usually double-tap or rotate to landscape).

Custom Channels
How do I add my own M3U8 stream? POPULAR

To add a custom channel:

  • Click the Settings (gear) icon in the top-right corner of the app.
  • Scroll to the "Add Your Channel" section.
  • Enter a Channel Name (e.g., "My Sports Channel").
  • Paste the M3U8 URL (must end in .m3u8 or .ts).
  • Select a Category or leave it as "Custom".
  • Check "Needs Proxy" if the stream requires CORS bypass.
  • Click "Add Channel".

Your custom channels are saved locally in your browser and will appear in the Live Channels list. You can remove them anytime from the same Settings panel.

Where can I find valid M3U8 URLs?

M3U8 URLs are streaming links used by IPTV services and live broadcasters. You can find them from:

  • Official broadcaster websites that offer HLS streams
  • IPTV subscription services (ensure you have the right to use them)
  • Publicly available stream indexes and directories

Important: Only add streams that you have the legal right to access. Yara Sport TV does not provide or endorse pirated content.

How do I delete a custom channel?

To remove a custom channel:

  • Open Settings (gear icon).
  • Scroll to the "Add Your Channel" section.
  • You'll see a list of your custom channels.
  • Click the "Remove" button next to the channel you want to delete.

Note: You cannot delete the default built-in channels — only custom channels you've added yourself.

Settings & Customization
How do I change the theme color?

To change the accent color:

  • Open Settings (gear icon).
  • Scroll to "Theme Color".
  • Click on any color circle to apply it instantly.

Available colors: Purple (default), Red, Green, Blue, Orange, Pink, Cyan, and Orange. Your choice is saved locally and persists across sessions.

Can I switch between Dark and Light mode?

Yes! In Settings → Theme Mode, you can choose:

  • Dark: Always use the dark theme (default).
  • Light: Always use the light theme.
  • Auto: Automatically switch based on your device's/system preference.

Your preference is saved and will be remembered the next time you open the app.

Troubleshooting
The player shows a black screen but I hear audio

This usually indicates a video decoding issue:

  • Try a different browser. Chrome and Firefox have the best HLS support.
  • Update your browser to the latest version.
  • Disable hardware acceleration in your browser settings (can sometimes conflict with HLS playback).
  • Try the Proxy mode if available for that channel — the direct stream may have encoding issues.
What keyboard shortcuts are available? NEW

Yara Sport TV supports the following keyboard shortcuts:

  • Space — Play/Pause
  • F — Toggle Fullscreen
  • M — Toggle Mute
  • P — Toggle Picture-in-Picture

Make sure the player is focused (click on it once) before using shortcuts.

How do I reset the app or clear saved data?

To clear all saved data (custom channels, theme preferences):

  • Chrome: Press F12 → Application tab → Local Storage → Select this site → Right-click → Clear.
  • Firefox: Press F12 → Storage tab → Local Storage → Select this site → Delete All.
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data → Search for the site → Remove.

Alternatively, you can clear your browser's cache and cookies for this site. This will reset all preferences and remove custom channels.
